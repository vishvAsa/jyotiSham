APPENDIX V: BIBLIOGRAPHY

  1. Āryabhaṭīya of Āryabhaṭa (b. A.D. 476):
    • Cr. ed. with Intro., Eng. Tr., Notes, Comments and four Indexes, by K. S. Shukla and K. V. Sarma, Indian National Sc. Academy, New Delhi, 1976.
    • Cr. ed. with the commentary of Bhāskara I, with Intro. and nine Appendices, by K. S. Shukla, Indian National Sc. Academy, New Delhi, 1976.
    • Cr. ed. with the commentary of Sūryadeva-yajvan, with Intro. and three Appendices, by K. V. Sarma, Indian National Sc. Academy, New Delhi, 1976.
    • Pt. I. Gaṇitapāda and Pt. II. Kālakriyāpāda, ed. with the com. entitled Mahābhāṣya of Nīlakaṇṭha Somasutvan, by K. Sambasiva Śāstrī, Trivandrum, 1930, 1931; Pt. III. Golapāda, ed. by Śūranāḍ Kuñjan Piḷḷai, 1957, (TSS, 101, 110, 185).
    • Tr. into Eng. by P. C. Sengupta, J of the Deptt. of Letters, Calcutta Univ., 16 (1927) 1–56.
  2. Bhāgavat-Purāṇa:
    • Pub. Gita Press, Gorakhpur, Sam. 1999.
  3. Brahmasphuṭasiddhānta of Brahmagupta (A.D. 628):
    • Ed. by Sudhākara Dvivedī with his own com., Benares, 1902.
    • Ed. with Sudhākara’s com. and his own com. Vāsanā and Hindi Tr. by Ram Swarup Sharma, with Introduction by Satya Prakash, 4 vols., New Delhi, 1966.
  4. Bṛhatsaṃhitā of Varāhamihira (d. A.D. 587):
    • Ed. with the com. of Bhaṭṭotpala, by Sudhākara Dvivedī, 2 vols., Benares, 1895–97, (Vizianagaram Skt. Ser., No. 10); by Avadh Bihari Tripathi, 1968, (Sarasvati Bhavana Granthamālā, No. 97).
    • (Tr. in Eng): Complete system of Natural Astrology, by H. Kern, Verspreide Geschriften (Collected Works), ’s-Gravenhage, 1913.
    • Ed. with Eng. Tr. by V. Subrahmanya Sastri and M. Ramakrishna Bhat, 2 vols., Bangalore, 1947.
  5. Candracchāyāgaṇita of Nīlakaṇṭha Somayāji:
    • Cr. ed. with Auto-commentary, Introduction, Translation and two Appendices, by K. V. Sarma, Hoshiarpur, 1976.
  6. Dṛggaṇita of Parameśvara (A.D. 1360–1455):
    • Cr. ed. with Intro., and Appendix by K. V. Sarma, V.V.R. Inst., Hoshiarpur, 1963.
  7. Goladīpikā (1) of Parameśvara (A.D. 1360–1455):
    • Ed. by T. Ganapati Sastri, Trivandrum, 1916, (TSS, No. 49).
  8. Goladīpikā (2) of Parameśvara (A.D. 1360–1455):
    • Cr. ed. with auto-com., Intro. and Tr., by K. V. Sarma, Adyar, 1957.
  9. Golasāra of Nīlakaṇṭha Somayāji (A.D. 1444–1545):
    • Cr. ed. with Intro. and Tr., by K. V. Sarma, Hoshiarpur, 1970.
  10. Grahacāranibandhana of Haridatta (c. A.D. 650–700):
    • Cr. ed. with Intro. and App., Grahacāranibandhana-saṃgraha, by K. V. Sarma, K.S.R. Inst., Madras, 1954.
  11. Kālanirṇaya (Kālamādhava) of Sāyaṇa-Mādhava:
    • Ed. by Ratna Gopal Bhatta, Benares, 1909.
  12. Karaṇaratna of Deva (A.D. 689):
    • Cr. ed. with Intro., Tr. and Notes etc., by K. S. Shukla. Ms.
  13. Kāśikā-vṛtti with Padamañjarī of Haradatta:
    • Ed. by Dwarikadasa Shastri and Kalikaprasada Shukla, Prachya Bharati Prakasan, Varanasi, 6 vols., 1965 ff.
  14. Khaṇḍakhādyaka of Brahmagupta (A.D. 665):
    • Ed. with Vāsanābhāṣya of Āmarāja, ed. by Babuaji Misra, Calcutta Univ., 1925.
    • Ed. with the com. of Caturveda Pṛthūdakasvāmin (A.D. 864), ed. by P. C. Sengupta, Calcutta Univ., 1941.
    • Cr. ed. with the com. of Bhaṭṭotpala and Tr. in Eng., by Bina Chatterji, 2 vols., World Press, Calcutta, 1970.
  15. Laghu-Bhāskarīya of Bhāskara I (A.D. 629):
    • Cr. ed with the com. Vivaraṇa of Śaṅkaraṇārāyaṇa, Trivandrum, 1949, (TSS, No. 162).
    • Ed. with the com. of Parameśvara, Poona, (An.SS, No. 128).
    • Cr. ed. with Tr. and Intro., by K. S. Shukla, Lucknow Univ., 1963.
  16. Laghumānasā of Muñjāla (A.D. 932):
    • Ed. with com. of Parameśvara, Poona, 1944, (An. SS, No. 123).
    • Ed. with Tr. and critical notes, by N. K. Majumdar; Calcutta, 1951.
    • Cr. edn, with com. Vāsanābhāṣya of Sūryadeva-yajvan, by K. V. Sarma, Ms.
  17. Līlāvatī of Bhāskara II (b. A.D. 1114):
    • Cr. ed. with Kriyākramakarī, an elaborate exposition of the rationale, by Śaṅkara (A.D. 1534) and Nārāyaṇa (A.D. 1607), with Intro. and five Appendices by K. V. Sarma, Hoshiarpur, 1975.
    • Ed. with Colebrooke’s Tr. with Notes, by Haran Chandra Banerji, 2nd edn., Calcutta, 1927.
  18. Mahā-Bhāskarīya of Bhāskara I (A.D. 629):
    • Cr. ed. with the Bhāṣya of Govindasvāmin and the supercommentary Siddhāntadīpikā by Parameśvara, with Intro. and Appendices, by T. S. Kuppanna Sastri, Madras, 1957, (Madras Govt. Or. Ser., No. 130).
    • Ed. with the com. Karmadīpikā by Parameśvara, Poona, 1945, (An.SS, No. 126).
    • Cr. ed. and Tr. into., with Intro., by K. S. Shukla, Lucknow, 1960.
  19. Mahābhāṣya of Patañjali with Pradīpa of Kaiyaṭa:
    • Ed. by N. S. Press, Bombay, 1908.
  20. Pañcasiddhāntikā of Varāhamihira (d. A.D. 587):
    • Ed. with an original com. in Skt. by Sudhākara Dvivedī and Eng. Tr. by G. Thibaut, Benares, 1889; Rep., Motilal Banarsidas, Benares, 1930; Rep., Varanasi, 1968, (Chaukhamba Skt. Studies, No 68).
    • Pt. I. Text and Tr., by D. Pingree; Pt. II. Com. (in Eng.), by O. Neugebauer and D. Pingree, Royal Danish Academy of Sciences and Letters, Copenhagen, 1970–71.
  21. Siddhāntadarpaṇa of Nīlakaṇṭha Somayāji (A.D. 1444–1545):
    • Cr. ed with Tr. and App., by K. V. Sarma, Hoshiarpur, 1976.
  22. Siddhāntasekhara of Śrīpati (A.D. 1039):
    • Ed. with com. of Makkibhaṭṭa (for chs. 1–4) and com. of Babuaji Misra (for chs 5–20), 2 Pts., Calcutta Univ., 1932, 1947.
  23. Śiṣyadhīvṛddhida of Lalla (or Ralla) (A.D. 8th cent.):
    • Ed. by Sudhākara Dvivedi, Benares, 1886.
    • With the com. of Mallikārjuna Sūri (A.D. 1178), Cr. ed. by Bina Chatterji, National Sc. Academy, New Delhi, 1977.
    • With the com. of Bhāskara II (b. A.D. 1114). Ms.
  24. Sūrya-siddhānta (bet. A.D. 650–950):
    • Ed. with the com. Vivaraṇa of Parameśvara, and Cr. Intro., by K. S. Shukla, Lucknow Univ., 1957.
    • Tr. with Notes by E. Burgess and W. D. Whitney, J Am. Or. Soc., 6 (1859–60) 141–498; Sep. rep. of above, Johnson Reprint, New York, 1964; Rep. ed. with Intro., by P. C. Sengupta, Calcutta Univ., 1935.
  25. Taittirīya-Āraṇyaka:
    • Ed. with Bhāṣya of Sāyaṇa-Mādhava, Anandasrama Press, Poona, 1898, (An.SS, No. 36).
  26. Tantrasaṅgraha of Nīlakaṇṭha Somayāji (A.D. 1444–1545):
    • Ed. with the com. Laghuvivṛti of Śaṅkara Variyar, Trivandrum, 1958, (TSS, No. 188).
    • Text with Yuktidīpikā and Laghuvivṛti of Śaṅkara (An elaborate exposition of the rationale of Hindu astronomy). Cr. ed. with Intro. and four Appendices, by K. V. Sarma, Hoshiarpur, 1977.
  27. Tantravārttika of Kumārila Bhaṭṭa:
    • Ed. with com. Nyāyasudhā of Someśvara, Benares, 1901–9, (Ch.SS, No. 14).
  28. Vākyakaraṇa, ascribed to Vararuci (c. A.D. 1300):
    • Cr. ed. with com. Laghuprakāśikā of Sundararāja, with Intro. and App., by T. S. Kuppanna Sastri and K. V. Sarma, K.S.R. Inst., Madras-4, 1962.
  29. Yuktibhāṣā (Mal.) of Jyeṣṭhadeva:
    • Ed. with Notes by Rama Varma Maru Thampuran and A. R. Akhileśvara Iyer, Mangalodayam, Trichur, Pt. I, 1949.
    • Ed. with Notes by K. V. Sarma, Pt. II. Ms.
