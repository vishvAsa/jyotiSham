APPENDIX III: GLOSSARY OF TECHNICAL TERMS

  • अंश (aṃśa): Part; numerator; degree.
  • अक्ष (akṣa): Latitude.
  • अक्षज्या (akṣajyā): Rsine latitude.
  • अक्षोन्नति (akṣonnati): Terrestrial latitude.
  • अग्र (agra): Amplitude at rising (i.e., the north-south distance of the rising point from the east-west line; or the Rsine thereof).
  • अङ्गुल (aṅgula): Linear measure, inch, digit.
  • अधिक (adhika): Additive.
  • अनुलोम (anuloma): Direct motion (opposite of vakra).
  • अयनचलन (ayanacalana): Precession of the equinoxes.
  • अवनति (avanati): Zenith distance; parallax in latitude.
  • अवम (avama) (= तिथिक्षय): Omitted lunar day.
  • अविशेष-कर्म (aviśeṣa-karma): Successive approximation, process of iteration.
  • असु (asu) (= प्राण): Unit of time equal to 4 seconds.
  • अस्तमय (astamaya): Setting (diurnal or heliacal).
  • अहर्गण (ahargaṇa) (= द्युगण): Days elapsed from epoch.
  • अहोरात्रवृत्त (ahorātravṛtta) (= द्युवृत्त): Diurnal circle.
  • आक्ष (ākṣa): Sidereal.
  • अन्त्य (antya): Last (term).
  • अन्त्यद्युज्या (antyadyujyā): Maximum day radius.
  • अपक्रम (apakrama) (= क्रान्ति): Declination, obliquity of the ecliptic.
  • अपक्रमज्या (apakramajyā) (= क्रान्तिज्या): Sine declination.
  • अपमण्डल (apamaṇḍala) (= अपक्रममण्डल): Ecliptic.
  • अयन (ayana): Northward (उत्तर) and southward (दक्षिण) motion of the Sun or other planets.
  • आर्धरात्रिक (ārdharātrika): Beginning from midnight.
  • इच्छा (icchā) (= इच्छाराशि): Requisition, being one of the three quantities in the Rule of Three.
  • इच्छाफल (icchāphala): Fruit corresponding to icchā.
  • इन्दुपात (indupāta): Ascending node of the Moon.
  • इन्दूच्च (indūcca): Higher apsis (apogee) of the Moon.
  • इष्ट (iṣṭa): Desired or given number.
  • इष्टज्या (iṣṭajyā): Rsine at the desired point on the circumference of a circle.
  • उच्च (ucca): Higher apsis, especially pertaining to the epicycle of the equation of the centre.
  • उच्चनीचवृत्त (uccanīcavṛtta): Epicycle.
  • उत्क्रमज्या (utkramajyā): Rversed sine.
  • उत्तरविषुवत् (uttaraviṣuvat): Vernal equinox.
  • उत्तरायण (uttarāyaṇa): Northward motion of the Sun.
  • कक्ष्यामण्डल (kakṣyāmaṇḍala): Mean orbit, deferent, concentric.
  • करण (karaṇa): Astronomical manual, with a contemporary epoch.
  • कर्क (karka): Sign Cancer (Kaṭaka, Karkaṭa).
  • कर्ण (karṇa): Hypotenuse.
  • कला (kalā): Minute of arc.
  • कलार्धज्याः (kalārdhajyāḥ): The 24 Rsine differences in terms of minutes.
  • कलाव्यास (kalāvyāsa): Angular diameter in minutes.
  • कलि (kali): The aeon (yuga) which commenced on Feb 18, 3102 B.C.
  • कल्यब्द (kalyabda): Number of years elapsed from the beginning of the Kali epoch.
  • कल्प (kalpa): A period of 1000 yugas, equal to 4.32 billion solar years.
  • कल्यादि (kalyādi): Commencing from the Kali epoch.
  • कल्यादि ध्रुव (kalyādi-dhruva): Zero positions of planets at the commencement of the Kali epoch.
  • काललग्न (kālalagna): Time-rising point.
  • कृति (kṛti): Square.
  • केन्द्र (kendra): Anomaly; centre of a circle.
  • कोटि (koṭi): Vertical side of a right-angled triangle; complement of bhuja.
  • कोटिज्या (koṭijyā): Rsine koṭi or Rcosine bhuja.
  • क्रमज्या (kramajyā): Sum of the sine segments taken in order.
  • क्रान्ति (krānti) (= अपक्रम): Declination.
  • क्रान्तिज्या (krāntijyā): Rsine declination.
  • क्रान्तिमण्डल (krāntimaṇḍala): Zodiacal circle.
  • क्रिय (kriya): Sign Aries (Meṣa).
  • क्षितिज (kṣitija): Horizon.
  • क्षितिज्या (kṣitijyā): Earth-sine.
  • क्षेत्र (kṣetra): Geometrical figure.
  • क्षेत्रफल (kṣetraphala): Area.
  • क्षेप (kṣepa) (= नति): Celestial latitude; additive quantity.
  • क्षेप्य (kṣepya): Additive quantity.
  • ग्रह (graha): Planet, heavenly body.
  • खगोल (khagola): Sphere of the sky.
  • खण्डग्रहण (khaṇḍagrahaṇa): Partial eclipse.
  • खण्डज्या (khaṇḍajyā): Sine segment.
  • खमध्य (khamadhya): Zenith.
  • खेचर (khecara): Planet, heavenly body.
  • गणित (gaṇita): Mathematics.
  • गत (gata): Elapsed number or portion of days, nāḍīs, prāṇas, etc.
  • गतिकला (gatikālā): Motion in terms of minutes.
  • गतिविशेष (gativiśeṣa): Difference in motion.
  • गुण (guṇa) (= ज्या): Rsine.
  • गुणकार (guṇakāra): Multiplier.
  • गुणन (guṇana): Multiplication.
  • गुण्य (guṇya): Multiplicand.
  • गुर्वक्षर (gurvakṣara): Unit of time equal to 2/5 of a second (or 4 prāṇas).
  • गोल (gola): Sphere; celestial sphere.
  • ग्रहगति (grahagati): Motion or daily motion of a planet.
  • ग्रहण (grahaṇa): Eclipse.
  • ग्रहयुद्ध (grahayuddha): Planetary war, conjunction of planets with specific characteristics.
  • ग्रहयोग (grahayoga): Conjunction of two planets.
  • ग्रास (grāsa): Obscuration; measure of eclipse.
  • ग्राहक (grāhaka): Eclipsing body.
  • ग्राह्य (grāhya): Eclipsed body.
  • घटिका (ghaṭikā): Unit of time equal to 24 minutes.
  • घटिकामण्डल (ghaṭikāmaṇḍala): Celestial equator.
  • घन (ghana): Cube.
  • घनमध्य (ghanamadhya): Centre of a sphere.
  • चक्रकला (cakrakalā): Minutes contained in a circle (21600).
  • चक्रांश (cakrāṃśa): Degrees contained in a circle (360).
  • चतुर्युग (caturyuga): The four aeons combined (4,320,000 years).
  • चन्द्रग्रहण (candragrahaṇa): Lunar eclipse.
  • चन्द्रोच्च (candrocca): Higher apsis (apogee) of the Moon.
  • चर (cara): Declinational ascensional difference.
  • चरज्या (carajyā): Rsine of caradala.
  • चरदल (caradala) (= चरार्ध): Half ascensional difference.
  • चरप्राण (caraprāṇa): Prāṇas of ascensional difference.
  • चान्द्र (cāndra): Lunar.
  • चाप (cāpa): Arc.
  • चार (cāra): Motion.
  • छादक (chādaka) (= ग्राहक): Eclipsing body.
  • छाद्य (chādya) (= ग्राह्य): Eclipsed body.
  • छाया (chāyā): Shadow; Rsine of zenith distance.
  • छेद (cheda): Denominator.
  • जीवा (jīvā) (= ज्या): Rsine.
  • जूक (jūka): Sign Libra (Tulā).
  • ज्या (jyā): Rsine.
  • ज्याखण्ड (jyākhaṇḍa): Sine segment; Rsine difference.
  • ज्योतिश्चक्र (jyotiścakra): Circle of asterisms.
  • तन्त्र (tantra): Astronomical manual starting from the epoch of a yuga.
  • तमस् (tamas): Node of the Moon (especially ascending node).
  • ताराग्रह (tārāgraha): Star planets (Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn).
  • तिथि (tithi): Lunar day.
  • तुङ्ग (tuṅga) (= उच्च): Higher apsis (apogee).
  • तुला (tulā): Sign Libra.
  • त्रिज्या (trijyā): Radius of the standard circle (3438’).
  • त्रिभुज (tribhuja): Triangle.
  • त्रैराशिक (trairāśika): Rule of Three.
  • दक्षिणायन (dakṣiṇāyana): Southward motion of the Sun.
  • दक्षिणोत्तरमण्डल (dakṣiṇottaramaṇḍala): Meridian circle.
  • दक्षिणोत्तररेखा (dakṣiṇottararekhā): Meridian line.
  • दल (dala): Half.
  • दिनभुक्ति (dinabhukti): Daily motion.
  • दिव्यवर्ष (divyavarṣa): Divine year (equal to 360 human years).
  • दृक्कर्म (dṛkkarma): Reduction to observation.
  • दृक्क्षेप (dṛkkṣepa): Zenith distance of the nonagesimal or its Rsine.
  • दृक्क्षेपज्या (dṛkkṣepajyā): Rsine drkkṣepa.
  • दृक्क्षेपमण्डल (dṛkkṣepamaṇḍala): Vertical circle through the nonagesimal.
  • दृक्क्षेपलग्न (dṛkkṣepalagna): Nonagesimal.
  • दृग्गति (dṛggati): Rsine of the altitude of the nonagesimal.
  • दृग्गोल (dṛggola): Visible celestial sphere.
  • दृग्ज्या (dṛgjyā): Rsine of the zenith distance.
  • दृग्वृत्त (dṛgvṛttā): Vertical circle.
  • देशान्तर (deśāntara): Terrestrial longitude; correction for terrestrial longitude.
  • देशान्तरकाल (deśāntarakāla): Time difference due to terrestrial longitude.
  • देशान्तरसंस्कार (deśāntarasaṃskāra): Correction for local longitude.
  • दोर्ज्या (dorjyā): Rsine of the bhuja.
  • द्युगण (dyugaṇa): Days elapsed since epoch.
  • द्युगत (dyugata): Time elapsed from sunrise.
  • द्युज्या (dyujyā): Day radius.
  • द्युदल (dyudala): Midday.
  • द्युवृत्त (dyuvṛttā) (= अहोरात्रवृत्त): Diurnal circle.
  • धन (dhana): Positive; additive.
  • धनुः (dhanuḥ): Arc.
  • ध्रुव (dhruva): Celestial pole; zero position of a planet at epoch.
  • ध्रुवोन्नति (dhruvonnati): Elevation of the celestial pole.
  • नक्षत्र (nakṣatra): Star, asterism.
  • नक्षत्रदिन (nakṣatradina): Sidereal day.
  • नत (nata): Zenith distance.
  • नतज्या (natajyā): Rsine of zenith distance.
  • नतनाडी (natanāḍī): Interval in nāḍīs from midday.
  • नतांश (natāṃśa): Degrees of zenith distance.
  • नतशिञ्जिनी (nataśiñjinī) (= नतज्या): Rsine zenith distance.
  • नति (nati): Parallax in latitude.
  • नाडिका (nāḍikā) (= नाडी, घटिका): Unit of time (24 minutes).
  • निपात (nipāta): Immersion (in eclipse).
  • निरक्ष (nirakṣa): Terrestrial equator.
  • निरक्षक्षितिज (nirakṣakṣitija): Equatorial horizon.
  • नीच (nīca): Perigee or perihelion.
  • नीचोच्चवृत्त (nīcovṛtta): Epicycle.
  • पक्ष (pakṣa): Half of a lunar month.
  • पद (pada): Quadrant of a circle.
  • पदच्छाया (padacchāyā) (= पदमा): Gnomonic shadow measured in feet.
  • परमग्रास (paramagrāsa): Maximum obscuration in an eclipse.
  • परमापक्रम (paramāpakrama): Maximum declination (24°).
  • परिधि (paridhi): Circumference.
  • परिलेख (parilekha): Diagram, graphical representation.
  • परीक्षा (parīkṣā): Experimentation, observation.
  • पर्यय (paryaya) (= भगण): Revolutions of a planet in a yuga.
  • पर्व (parva) (= पर्वान्त): End point of the new/full moon.
  • पलज्या (palajyā): Rsine latitude.
  • पलभा (palabhā): Equinoctial shadow.
  • पात (pāta): Node (especially ascending node).
  • पूर्वापर (pūrvāpara): Prime vertical; east-west line.
  • पृष्ठ (pṛṣṭha): Surface.
  • प्रग्रह (pragraha): First contact in an eclipse.
  • प्रतिमण्डल (pratimaṇḍala): Eccentric circle.
  • प्रमाण (pramāṇa): Argument in the Rule of Three.
  • प्राग्लग्न (prāglagna): Ascendant.
  • प्राण (prāṇa): Unit of time equal to 4 sidereal seconds.
  • प्राणकलान्तर (prāṇakalāntara): Difference in minutes expressed in prāṇas.
  • फल (phala): Fruit in the Rule of Three; equation.
  • बाहु (bāhu) (= भुज): Lateral side of a right-angled triangle.
  • बिम्ब (bimba): Disc of a celestial body.
  • बीज (bīja): Astronomical correction applied to mean planets.
  • भगण (bhagaṇa): Revolutions of a planet in a yuga.
  • भगोल (bhagola): Sphere of asterisms, celestial sphere.
  • भचक्र (bhacakra): Ecliptic circle.
  • भाग (bhāga) (= अंश): Degree.
  • भुक्ति (bhukti): Daily motion.
  • भुज (bhuja) (= भुजा): Side of a right-angled triangle; longitude in a quadrant.
  • भुजज्या (bhujajyā) (= दोर्ज्या): Rsine of bhuja.
  • भुजान्तरफल (bhujāntaraphala): Correction for equation of time due to eccentricity.
  • भुजाफल (bhujāphala): Equation of the centre.
  • भू (bhū) (= भूमि): Earth.
  • भूगोल (bhūgola): Sphere of the Earth.
  • भूच्छाया (bhūcchāyā): Earth’s shadow.
  • भूदिन (bhūdina) (= सावनदिन): Terrestrial civil day.
  • भूभ्रम (bhūbhrama): Rotation of the Earth.
  • भूवृत्त (bhūvṛttā): Circumference of the Earth.
  • भोग (bhoga): Motion; position.
  • मकर (makara) (= मृग): Sign Capricorn.
  • मण्डल (maṇḍala): Circle; orb.
  • मध्यगति (madhyagati): Mean motion.
  • मध्यग्रहण (madhyagrahaṇa): Middle of eclipse.
  • मध्यच्छाया (madhyacchāyā): Midday shadow.
  • मध्यम (madhyama): Mean planet / position.
  • मन्द (manda): Slow; apogee (mandocca).
  • मन्दकर्म (mandakarma): Manda operation in planetary computation.
  • मन्दकेन्द्र (mandakendra): Manda anomaly.
  • मन्दफल (mandaphala): Equation of the centre.
  • मन्दपरिधि (mandaparidhi): Epicycle of the equation of the centre.
  • मन्दोच्च (mandocca): Apogee or aphelion.
  • महाच्छाया (mahācchāyā): Rsine of zenith distance.
  • महाशङ्कु (mahāśaṅku): Rsine of altitude.
  • मुहूर्त (muhūrta): Unit of time equal to 48 minutes.
  • मूल (mūla) (= वर्गमूल): Square root.
  • मेषादि (meṣādi): First point of Aries.
  • मोक्ष (mokṣa) (= विमोक्ष): Last contact / emersion in an eclipse.
  • मौढ्य (mauḍhya): Combustion of a planet.
  • याम्य (yāmya): Southern.
  • याम्योत्तररेखा (yāmyottararekhā): Meridian line.
  • युग (yuga): Aeon.
  • युग्म (yugma): Even quadrant.
  • योग (yoga): Conjunction of planets.
  • योजन (yojana): Unit of distance (approx. 7.5 miles).
  • योजनव्यास (yojanavyāsa): Diameter in yojanas.
  • राशि (rāśi): Sign of the zodiac (30°).
  • राशिकूट (rāśikūṭa): Nonagesimal point of the ecliptic.
  • राशिगोल (rāśigola) (= भगोल): Celestial sphere.
  • राहु (rāhu) (= पात): Ascending node of the Moon.
  • लग्न (lagna): Rising point of the ecliptic.
  • लङ्कोदय (laṅkōdaya): Right ascension of signs.
  • लम्ब (lamba): Co-latitude; Rsine of co-latitude.
  • लम्बन (lambana): Parallax in longitude.
  • लिप्ता (liptā) (= कला): Minute of arc.
  • लिप्ताव्यास (liptāvyāsa): Angular diameter in minutes.
  • वक्र (vakra): Retrograde motion.
  • वर्ग (varga): Square.
  • वर्गमूल (vargamūla): Square root.
  • विलोम (viloma): Retrograde.
  • विक्षेप (vikṣepa): Celestial latitude.
  • विमण्डल (vimaṇḍala) (= विक्षेपमण्डल): Orbit of a planet.
  • विनाडिका (vināḍikā) (= चषक): Unit of time equal to 24 seconds (1/60 of a nāḍī).
  • विमर्द (vimarda): Duration of total eclipse.
  • विवर (vivara): Difference.
  • विशेष (viśeṣa): Difference.
  • विषम (viṣama): Odd quadrant.
  • विषुवत् (viṣuvat): Equinox.
  • विषुवच्छाया (viṣuvacchāyā): Equinoctial midday shadow.
  • वृत्त (vṛttā): Circle.
  • व्यास (vyāsa): Diameter.
  • व्यासार्ध (vyāsārdha): Radius.
  • शङ्कु (śaṅku): Gnomon; Rsine of altitude.
  • शङ्कुकोटि (śaṅkukoṭi): Rsine of zenith distance.
  • शर (śara): Arrow; Rversed sine.
  • शिष्ट (śiṣṭa) (= शेष): Remainder.
  • शीघ्र (śīghra): Fast; śīghrocca.
  • शीघ्रकर्ण (śīghrakarṇa): Geocentric vector.
  • शीघ्रकर्म (śīghrakarma): Planetary equation of conjunction.
  • शीघ्रोच्च (śīghrocca): Apex of conjunction.
  • शून्य (śūnya): Zero.
  • शोध्य (śodhya): Subtractive quantity.
  • संवर्ग (saṃvarga) (= गुणन): Multiplication.
  • संक्रान्ति (saṃkrānti): Transit of the Sun into a zodiacal sign.
  • सममण्डल (samamaṇḍala): Prime vertical.
  • सममण्डलच्छाया (samamaṇḍalacchāyā): Shadow when the Sun is on the prime vertical.

  • सायन (sāyana): With precession added.
  • सावनदिन (sāvanadina): Civil day (sunrise to sunrise).
  • सूर्यग्रहण (sūryagrahaṇa): Solar eclipse.
  • सौम्य (saumya): Northern.
  • सौर (saura): Solar.
  • स्थित्यर्ध (sthityardha): Half-duration of an eclipse.
  • स्फुट (sphuṭa): True position / value.
  • स्फुटगति (sphuṭagati): True daily motion.
  • स्फुटनति (sphuṭanati): True parallax in latitude.
  • स्फुटविक्षेप (sphuṭavikṣepa): True celestial latitude corrected for parallax.
  • क्षेप (kṣepa): Additive quantity.
  • स्वदेशराशिमान (svadeśarāśimāna): Local oblique ascension of a sign.
