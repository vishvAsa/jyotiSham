So far, all Earth-size rocky planets that have been targeted by JWST, mostly planets around red dwarfs (UV Ceti stars), appear to have been stripped free of their atmospheres – they are just baked bare rocks like Mercury. None of this is very appetizing in terms of finding Earth twins.
